About Me


I’m a Software Engineer who believes in hard work, has a passion for all things programming, and is always willing to learn more.

Simplicity – the art of maximizing the amount of work not done – is essential.

From the Agile Manifesto

I began programming in high school and was interested in computers and technology even earlier than that. My dream of becoming a game developer was what originally drove me to go into software engineering, but I no longer limit myself to only this goal. I love all aspects of software development and love the challenges it brings.

I’ve practiced the various stages of software development, design, and testing. I’ve dabbled in game development, including design and programing. I’ve studied up on mobile development and applications trends. I’ve had both academic and professional experience when it comes to development. With all this knowledge and experience, I still can’t get enough technology in my life!
